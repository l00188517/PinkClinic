Debunking common lies about birth control

While women desire to get pregnant only when they want to, some myth and misconception surrounding the use of birth control have deterred them from choosing the right contraceptive that fit their body and lifestyle. This article demystifies those common lies you heard about birth control

  1. Birth control are for married women only- I always say to my client, as long as you are having sex and you are not ready to get pregnant, birth controls are safe for you and every other woman.
  2. Birth control causes weight gain- Weight gain from birth control if any, is usually less than 3kg. Eating healthy and exercising keeps this off. Moreover, there are hormone free birth control that you can choose form that wont chnage anything about your weight.
  3. Birth control causes Infertility- Birth control just helps you control the rate at which you get pregnant, it doesn't cause you to be sterile. Once you stop you can get pregnant almost immediately in some cases.
  4. Birth control makes women promiscuous- this has nothing to do with the sexual exploitation of a woman. Research had shown that women with birth control are more committed to their relationship and have fewer sexual partners.
  5. Birth control can cause cancer or fibriod- birth control dont typically cause you to have cancer or fibriod if used correctly and as directed. It is very important that you speak to a health care provider before choosing a birth control. A medical eligibility assessment is carried out to be sure there are no underlying conditions that can complicated your health.

All contraceptives are safe and equally effective to use, you just need to speak to the experts to be sure you are making the best choice that suits you and your lifestyle
