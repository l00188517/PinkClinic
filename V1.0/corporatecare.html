Birth Control/Family planning services- The choice is completely yours

Birth control is for any woman from age 16 years who is sexually active and not ready for an unwanted pregnancy or a baby mama drama. There are different types of modern birth control devices you can choose from.

Some birth control devices are made with hormones that stops you from ovulating so that you do not get pregnant while some are without hormones that helps you prevent pregnancy without altering anything in your body. All contraceptives are effective, you will need to visit the clinic for a proper assessment to know which type of contraceptive will be best for you.

Birth control available at the Pink clinic

  • Condoms
  • Diaphragm
  • Emergency contraceptive pills
  • Daily contraceptive pills
  • Contraceptive injection (3- monthly)
  • Sub dermal implant
  • Intrauterine contraceptive device (copper IUD)
  • Intrauterine system (Hormonal IUD)

Condom:

  • 98% effective
  • Can help you prevent pregnancy
  • It protect you from some STIs for up to 85%

Emergency Contraceptive pills

  • An emergency pill to be used once in a while. it's helps to prevent unwanted babies. It works by stopping you from ovulationg so you don’t get pregnant
  • It is 87% effective.

Injections:

  • 3 monthly injection given in the uooer thigh or buttocks. You will need this every 3 month to continue preventing pregnancy.
  • Works by preventing the woman body from releasing eggs so that pregnancy does not occur
  • It also makes the lining of her womb thick so that the sperm cannot pass through to the eggs.
  • It is 99% effective.

Implants:

  • 99% effective
  • Every 3-5 years but can be removed at any time.
  • Inserted in the upper arm
  • Very private. No one can tell
  • It does not affect sex
  • It does not protect from STIs
  • Most women can use including women with sickle cell, HIV

IUD:

  • 99.7% effective
  • 5- 10 years to prevent pregnancy
  • Does not have any hormone
  • Can’t make you fat or thin
  • Works by reducing the motility of the sperm so that it cant get to the eggs
  • Very safe for most women including women with breast cancer, hypertension, diabetes or obesity
