Bacteria Vaginosis

A Non Sexual infection caused by an overgrowth of a bacteria or activities that disrupt the vagina PH like douching.

Treatment for BV is recommended for women with symptoms like abnormal (creamy) discharges, fishy odor or itching. We understand the terrible state these symptoms can put you in and If you are experiencing any of the above, the best thing to do is to visit the clinic, there will be no need take your partner along.

Candidiasis- Vulvovaginal

Uncomplicated Vagina candidasis is not usually acquired by sexual transmission. Most women will experience Candida (also known as yeast infection or thrush) at least once in their life time. Fungus are found in the walls of the vagina, an overgrowth of these will lead to candidiasis or yeast infection.

Causes of candida include :

Stress
Hormone imbalance
Douching
Unmanaged diabetes
Compromised immune system
Over use of antibiotics
High carb diet
Some form of contraceptive

How do I know it’s a yeast infection?

Sadly, most vagina infections manifest the same symptoms and signs. It’s best to take on the vagina swab test to be sure of the actual cause . Common symptoms as shown by women who have the yeast infection include- vulva or vagina itching Fishy smell. Burning sensation during urination or after sex Redness, soreness

Chlamydia

A sexually transmitted infection that occurs among men and women, young people between 16years and 29. Chlamydia usually affects the cervix and can cause a woman to bleed in between periods. Other symptoms include burning sensation during pee or sex, itching, watery discharges, foul smell.

Chlamydia if untreated can result into infertility, PID , ectopic pregnancies, sperm cell mortality

Birth Control /Family Planning Services

There are different types of modern birth control devices you can choose from. Birth control is for any woman who is sexually active from age 16 years and not ready for the baby mama drama.

Some birth control are made with hormones that stops you from ovulating so that you don't get pregnant and there are some without hormones that helps you prevent pregnancy without altering anything in the body. All contraceptive are effective, you may need to visit the clinic to be sure of the best one for you

Birth Control available at pink clinic

  • Condoms
  • Emergency Contraceptive Pills
  • Daily contraceptive pills
  • Contraceptive injection (3- monthly)
  • Sub- Dermal Implants
  • Intrauterine contraceptive device (Copper IUD)
  • Intrauterine system (Hormonal IUD)
