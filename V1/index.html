You don’t have to suffer in silence.

STIs cause you to be sick. And if left untreated, they damage your organs and body systems.
You experience discomfort with common symptoms like itching, sores and bumps around your private area. You may even experience pain while urinating or during sexual intercourse. (Note that some STIs don’t show any physical symptoms).
This can be embarrassing. You lose your self-esteem, become withdrawn from your partner(s), family, friends and the society. You feel imprisoned. And this may gradually lead you to a state of depression.
Worse still, is that you don’t know where to access reliable and professional treatment without being judged.
The truth is… You don’t have to suffer in silence. You deserve more.

Become free from Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs).

At Pink Clinic, we can help you achieve an infection-free status in several ways.

  • We listen actively and provide non-judgemental guidance and counselling. This helps manage the mental stress you experience, knowing your STI status.
  • We undertake thorough screenings you can rely on so we know exactly what we are dealing with.
  • Our treatment helps you become free from STIs. You restore your confidence so you can quickly get back to the life you have always known.
